Honduran Platanos Fritos (Fried Banana)

Description

Fried Banana or Fried Plantains is another one of the widely popular side dishes in Honduras.

These Honduran platanos fritos merge sweet and savory flavors in one dish!

The ripe plantains are cut into fourths, fried until golden brown, then served with sour cream.

Ingredients

  • 2 black plantains
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Cut off the ends of the plantain then slice the knife through the banana making a straight line from the end to the other end.
  2. Put oil into a frying pan or a deep fryer could be used and fry until golden brown.
  3. Drain on a paper towel
  4. Serve warm as part of the plato typico or with some sour cream as a lovely snack.
