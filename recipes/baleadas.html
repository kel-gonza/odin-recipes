Beleadas

Description

You may all know about Mexican Tamales, Salvadorean Pupusas and Italy's Lasagna. But, do you all know about Honduran Baleadas?

Honduran Baleadas is a well known traditional meal that is just made with home-made tortillas, sour cream,cheese, and mushed beans

Bellow I will give you the recipe of my mother's country food.

Enjoy!

Ingredients

Tortillas

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cups water
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
