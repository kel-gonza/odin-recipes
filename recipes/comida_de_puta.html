Comida de Puta (Bread with Sour Cream)

Description

Are you on a rush? Do you not have time to cook yourself some food?

Well here is a solution for your fast food remedies!

Comida de puta is a solution it is easy to make and you can literally make it in 2 minutes or less!

The actual translation is A Bitch's Food because bitches don't have time to eat

Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Bolillo(roll) Bread
  • Sour Cream(mexican version)

Instructions

  1. Get a Bolillo Bread and heat it if you like in the oven for 4 mins or until crusty
  2. (skip to step 3 if you are really lazy. I lowkey skip to step 3) After, bread is done being heated open the bread with a knife by parting it in the middle.
  3. Put sour cream in the middle or if you skipped part 2 dip the bread inside the sour cream
  4. eat
